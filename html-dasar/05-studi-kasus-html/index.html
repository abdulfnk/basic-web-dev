About Neon Nights

WPU StreamFest 2026 is a three-day music festival set in a vibrant neon nights atmosphere. Each day invites you to escape the ordinary and be fully present—immersed in the music, the energy, and the atmosphere, free from distractions.

Built with an eco-friendly approach, cashless payments, and a seamless digital experience, everything is designed to be simple, fast, and effortless—so you can stay in the moment and enjoy the festival to the fullest.
